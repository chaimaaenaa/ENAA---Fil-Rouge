Frequently Ask Questions

How does this program works?

This program works as follows:

Enroll in the program.
You will receive an confirmation email from team Kaish Fitness.
You will receive an email within 48 hours from our team in which all the meeting details with your coach are present.
After that meeting, your coach will add you to our coaching platform.
After the addition in our coaching platform, you will receive an email to set up your account on our platform.
After that within 48 hours, your assigned coach will provide you with a nutrition plan and workout plan which you need to follow everyday.
Time to time we will give you some tasks that will directly or indirectly help you in achieving your goal. E.g. Reduce sugar intake by 20%. Sleep at 11 pm max and get up early in the morning at 6 am for the next 21 days.
You have to log your workout and nutrition on a daily basis, it will help your coach in tracking your progress daily.
You need to upload your progress photos and measurements on selected days. (Our system will remind you on time to do so)
Assessment sessions (video) will be taken after every two weeks.
Your Nutrition/ Workout plan will be revised after analysing your results.
Note: You can text your coach anytime, whenever you want for any type of queries.
